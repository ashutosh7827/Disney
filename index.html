Disney World

Disneyland is an amusement park in Anaheim, California. It is the first of two theme parks built at the Disneyland Resort in Anaheim, California, and was opened on July 17, 1955. The park features characters, rides, and shows based on the creations of Walt Disney and the Disney Company. The central building of the park, the Sleeping Beauty Castle, is modeled on Germany’s Neuschwanstein Castle
